# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Software engineering internship recruiters and hiring managers. They scan quickly between many candidates, often on laptop, sometimes mobile. They need proof of backend/full-stack ability, project depth, and professional presentation without friction.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that communicates Weihao Lee as a credible software engineering intern candidate: clear identity, scannable projects with evidence, one-click CV download, and direct email contact. Success means a recruiter understands role fit, tech stack, and project impact within one minute and knows how to reach out.

## Brand Personality

Simple, clean, confident. Quiet agency polish (readable hierarchy, generous space) without creative-studio theatrics. Developer-first: projects and stack lead; no hobby distractions.

## Anti-references

Overly complex or flashy layouts that hide information. Generic dark "GitHub clone" dev portfolios. Cream/warm AI landing pages. Gradient text, glass cards, hero metrics, numbered section eyebrows, identical icon-card grids. Marketing buzzword copy.

## Design Principles

1. **Recruiter scan path** — Name, role, projects, contact visible without hunting.
2. **Show the work** — Project screenshots and galleries carry proof; copy stays short.
3. **Calm over clever** — Typography and spacing do the work; motion stays subtle.
4. **Practice what you preach** — The site itself demonstrates clean frontend craft.
5. **One action per beat** — Primary CTA per section (view work, download CV, email).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Semantic landmarks, visible focus, keyboard-navigable modals/carousels, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected, body text ≥4.5:1 contrast.
